The Inner Circle

OUR Mission

Is to Introduce and help students successfully navigate a seemingly difficult market (the Foreign Exchange market). We will highlight and simplify complex elements to provide a basic level of understanding that contributes to a reduction in time for students to see success in the market.

OUR Vision

Is to expose students early so that they might know the full potential of the market. We want to pique interest and lay a foundation for alternative sources of income. While we fully expect most to treat this as a launching pad for careers in banking, we look forward to expanding the knowledge base with a more complex thought processes for those who find they have a passion to immerse themselves in the market
